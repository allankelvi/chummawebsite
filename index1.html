About Me

Mechanical Engineer

I'm a Mechanical Engineer with a flair for innovation and a passion for technology, specializing in Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) where I'm fluent in ANSYS. My journey in the world of engineering has been a thrilling adventure, where I've honed my skills in front-end development, graphic design, and CAD drawing. I find solace in CAD drawing and design, transforming concepts into tangible realities. The dynamic blend of my mechanical engineering background, creative skills, and expertise in Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) allows me to provide excellent services to small enterprises, helping them navigate the digital landscape with an eye for detail and an obsession for quality.
